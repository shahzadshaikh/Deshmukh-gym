Nutrition

Gym nutrition is the foundation of your fitness results.

1.Pre-Workout (1–2 hours before):

Fueling up prevents hunger, provides quick energy, and stops digestive issues.

2.Intra-Workout (During training):

Focus entirely on hydration unless your workout pushes past \(60\) to \(90\) minutes.

3. Post-Workout (30–60 minutes after):

Your body is primed to absorb nutrients quickly to replenish glycogen stores and repair muscle fibers.

4. Daily Micronutrients & Hydration:

Water Intake: Drink water throughout the day. A standard rule of thumb is at least \(3\) to \(4\) liters daily to support metabolism and joint health.
