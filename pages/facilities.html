Facilities

A modern gym provides a combination of state-of-the-art workout equipment, dedicated training zones, and essential wellness amenities.

1. Cardio zone:

Equipped with treadmills, stationary bikes, ellipticals, and rowing machines for endurance training.

2. Strength Training Area

Features a mix of resistance machines, barbells, dumbbells, and cable crossovers for muscle building.

3. Functional Training & Free Weights:

Open spaces and turf areas utilizing kettlebells, medicine balls, kettlebells, and TRX suspension trainers.

4. Stretching & Flexibility Space:

Designated matted areas for yoga, pilates, warm-ups, and mobility work.
